# 🎓 MyCollegeBook | High-Fidelity Digital SAT® Preparation

Founder & Lead Developer of **[MyCollegeBook](https://mycollegebook.satprepin.com)** — a digital SAT simulator designed to eliminate test-day surprises.

### 🚀 What is MyCollegeBook?
Most students show up to the SAT having never practiced on a real digital interface. We built a platform that mirrors the **CollegeBoard Bluebook™ environment 1:1**, focusing on accessibility and deep behavioral analytics.

*   **⚡ 100% Web-Based:** Optimized for school-managed Chromebooks and laptops (no admin passwords or app installs required).
*   **🛠️ High-Fidelity Simulator:** Pixel-perfect recreation of the Digital SAT interface, including an integrated Desmos calculator and adaptive module logic.
*   **📊 Behavioral Insights:** We go beyond "Correct vs. Incorrect" to track your pacing, second-guessing patterns, and where you lose time under pressure.
*   **🆓 Accessible Prep:** We offer a full-length adaptive practice test for free to help every student know where they stand.

### 🛠️ Tech Stack & Development
I'm currently building the future of EdTech using:

*   **Backend:** Supabase (Auth, DB, Real-time)
*   **Deployment:** Vercel & CloudRun
*   **Logic:** Custom adaptive scoring engines mapped to official SAT scaled score tables.
